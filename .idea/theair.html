The Air

 

The ensemble starts on this particular line of blurred air that comes when, in a three-dimensional space, objects and colours superpose themselves in the distance. The reflection of the air on the extreme left of the mauve quilt creates an infinitesimal milky stain that quickly gets lost in the over-powering red wine shade which composes the blanket.
