The Beauty Spot

The beauty spot, as if drilled into the foundation of the skin, milky beige. The placement of the dress, under. An elevated state. An ashy thread attaches the straps. Every time the needle passed, the silk was transformed. It created little dots and a pointed wicked hat. The dress begins and already disappears, in the draperies, the curtains, on things the lady shelters. Transparent pink oozy cocktail, poured on the madam. A cloud at sunset, or the inside of a tackily-lit jacuzzi where the law mandates sex and evaporation.
