The Breasts

There is an elevation, a shift in colour, a brownie, something appealing, a muddy piece of wooden meat, a stain under the cover, a birds’ nest not a birds’ nest. There is something that altered and grew, precedently it did not form. In the folded versions of the lady there was no dimension. A curve. It is all that can be said. The extreme point, the nipple, rubs on the lavender and a small spire of light is derived. On the left, it hangs, it makes a pleat in the dress, a wrapped wire of cloth lying on the divan.
