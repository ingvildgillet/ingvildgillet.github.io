The Divan

Hair is scattered on foggy merlot and on another colour. That colour is blue, pastel, gleaming, baby, chewing gum, clear-oozy, grand-mother, violet, flower, dot, pink, with patterns, castles, old, pale alabaster, moon, flowy, birds singing, luminescent, tapestry twill, rabbit. This rich colour makes the sofa inviting. Sewn-in arches become the breaking point between the deepest hues of the fabric and the brown tint of the wood. They are multiple threaded versions of a reconciliation.
