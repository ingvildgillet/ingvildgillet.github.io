The Ears

Light irregularity also takes place inside the ears. More holes. Two but there is only one to be seen. It rests on the right side of her face. Skin comes like a secret. Hidden, hidden away by the blond spikes. The ear is more or less red, more or less snake. The lady with one ear. The ear is the border, at the extremity, coal walls. The ear is the passage and also a void in which you might fall. On the lobe, a bit further down, there are two cracks. The lady inflicted that on herself. It is her flesh. Following the ear, passing down the cheeks, under the nose, we can find something that always was important.
