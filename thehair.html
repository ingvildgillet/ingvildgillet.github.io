The Hair

The uniform colour of the blanket somehow fades under the power of attraction of scattered thin blond straws. And this is where the contradiction between what is attached and what is in the air may be best understood. The yellow strings of hair are either magnetized by the synthetic fabric, meandering like aimless snakes, or they stand in the void as indefinitely fragile, delicate objects.
