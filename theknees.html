The Knees

And now the big disco light in the club, a knee forming a bombshell, another one dripping. A box superior to the other greets the light politely. It will be destroyed first. One knee is beautiful, standing heavy and happy when the melting one is marbled cake. The beautiful leg is strong, it is chiselled by the muscles and the variation of what is sane and insane. The sun and colours are perceptible through the window. What is dark gives appetite. The second leg, because it rests, is soft. It creates a longing like sweets to salt.
