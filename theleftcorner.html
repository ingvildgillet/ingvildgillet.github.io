The Left Corner

At the left edge of the divan, is a triangle made of something like a bag of flesh, that is instead a mauve carpetlike blanket. The line at the extreme left almost sparkles from the contrast between the white wall and the ultramarine camera case and the green and black hard-cotton bag, hidden away by a blue swimsuit that was carelessly thrown on top of it. All of this results in the disappearance of that precise line, where the purple blanket rejoins the mahogany wooden foot of the divan. This corner encapsulates the very beginning of the portrait.
