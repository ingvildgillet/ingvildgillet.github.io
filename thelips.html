The Lips

At the extremities of the mouth, onyx skin like clouds falling slightly upside down. The string in the middle screaming open, threading volume of it all. An open wound. A swollen one. A puffy cushion and then a kiss. Found in the layers in the wrinkles of the mouth, a kiss to herself, a kiss discovered in the battle of the flesh, a kiss to milady. My lady, the lady.
