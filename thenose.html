The Nose

The nose is round. The nose began flat and grew taller. This generated disappointment on rainy days. But also, the nose is circular and evenly balanced and so it is a rope in the middle of the face, a butterfly. A beauty spot lies on the right corner of the snout. At the nose’s peak, the light bounces and creates snow. At the extremity where holes arise, some are filled with aspiration and secrecy and it is cooler inside them, maybe, maybe, there were hairy trolls that set camp in these humid caves. Maybe, maybe, it’s because all nanoparticles of light get lost on their way, doors, when darkness condenses. It really is a mystery, nostrils.
