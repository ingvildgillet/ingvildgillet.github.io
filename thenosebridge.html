The Nose Bridge

There is a stem of flesh like a salmon on a red sea, at the corner where spiders meet, and then a touch of nacre between the crowded reunion. The nose enters still completely flat. Walking it would be like strolling on a green valley facing the cliffs of the alps. Stripped shoelaces dancing the waltz. The essence is reached through the skin, the texture soft and pink, warm elastic.
