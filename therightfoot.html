The Right Foot

The foot attached to the resting leg is curved and is a dancer, a pincer, a crab. There is a juxtaposition of different skin complexions. An ankle which is an ankle in movement, freezes. The second leg cuts at the edge of the foot. A refuge. Tibialis anterior and extensor digitorum longus. A bruise on the leg echoes a dirty stain on the divan.
