The Right Forearm

The hand is an introduction to the lady’s bones. Metacarpophalangeal joints transform into hills of power and anger. At the radius, great cheerings, the ulna, to whom we say hello. They appear strongly, these bones, under the flesh, because of the way the lady holds her arm against her head. Because of the position that makes them twisted absolutely. Curved tables, some straws, a swing. The forearm follows, becomes cosmic and full of light, the softest of the softest. The colour tender and lighter. The lady’s forearm like an uncut wedding cake, foamy. One would like to place one’s teeth on that flesh and consume. Deep red blood. What is soft is exquisite.
