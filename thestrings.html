The Strings

Hair flying lonely, graceful creatures sad, almost imperceptible. A melancholy rises from these solitary spikes. One would expect hair to be singular and unified, and one would not. The power lies in the freedom of control, it lies in the resilience of strings standing above everything, surrounded by emptiness. Their cousins sisters brothers have been caught in the electrical field of the polar violet blanket. They are seen similar to cyclical shooting stars. The reflection of the light outside makes one point brighter, then another, sparkles following the rest of the hair like drops.
