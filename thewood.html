The Wood

But the wood is hoping to disappear. The wood is under and around. The wood does not penetrate nor touch what will become the subject. It surrounds and catches the light, functioning like the contour of a canvas. A thin blur rises from the junction of polished chocolate wood and the whiteness of the walls, the whiteness of flooring made of birch trees. How things are done in a Swedish living room. There is a strong distinction between what is wood and what is wall except where the mauve drapery encompassed objects on its fading line.
